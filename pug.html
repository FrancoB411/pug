Headers

In HTML5, the semantic value of a header tag is relative to its section. This means each <section> may have it's own <h1>. Some examples:

  • h2 = section h1
  • h3 = section h2
  • h3 = section section h1
  • h5 = section section section h2

When viewing the references below, remember to factor in whether the content area showing this styleguide is already wrapped in a <section> tag.
